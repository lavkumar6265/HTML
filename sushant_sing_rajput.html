Sushant Singh Rajput was an an Indian film and television actor. He
is best known for playing the role of Manav in the ongoing TV series,
Pavitra Rishta on Zee Tv.


Rajput was born in Patna where his father worked as a government official.

Small screen’s heartthrob, Sushant Singh Rajput entered Hindi films early this year. His
boy-bonding drama Kai Po Che (2013), directed by Abhishek Kapoor, based on Chetan
Bhagat‘s famous novel The Three Mistakes Of My Life, released on 22 February. His
chemistry with costar Ankita Lokhande from Pavitra Rishta was also well appreciated
by the audience. Sushant showed his acting prowess in the film and took the cine-circles
by storm. As a result, the handsome dude has lapped up PK with Aamir Khan and
Anushka Sharma, YRF’s next project with Parineeti Chopra, and Imtiaz Ali‘s next
film.
